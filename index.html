  • "mosquito net thread watter fall"

    Why is it called Mosquito Net Yarn? It is called benang kelambu waterfall because the water flow looks like thin threads. This can happen because the water flow is covered by dense plants that grow around the cliffs. This means that the flow of water that comes out is not heavy and dangles like a thread, so it doesn't make a loud noise

  • "Mount Rinjani"

    What is Mount Rinjani famous for? It is not surprising that Mount Rinjani is included in the ranks of Indonesia's Seven Summits and is often visited by local and international climbers. Mount Rinjani is famous for its natural charm which has a peak called Puncak Dewi Anjani, and a crater lake called Segara Anak.

  • "community service program"

    KKN is an abbreviation for Real Work Lectures. or what is called a "community service program". Broadly speaking, KKN is a student service activity for the community in accordance with the field of science they are studying. The aim of KKN is to provide an opportunity for students to apply their knowledge.

  • "semoyang village office"

    KKN at the Semoyang Village Office is not the end of a journey, but the beginning of a strong bond between students and the community. Here, they leave a mark that brings inspiration, hope and a spirit of goodness to the next generation.
